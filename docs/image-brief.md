# Image Brief — Plumber Stock Set v1

For generating the shared stock images the plumber niche pack uses when a business has no usable photos of their own (the majority). These render on **our generated websites only** — never uploaded to anyone's Google Business Profile (Google prohibits stock/AI imagery there).

## Global style

- Photorealistic, natural light, warm and trustworthy — working-class professional, not corporate stock-photo gloss.
- American residential settings. Modern but lived-in homes.
- Subjects: working hands, tools, fixtures, work vans, job scenes. **Avoid identifiable faces** (these represent many different businesses) — backs, hands, mid-torso crops are ideal.
- No visible text, logos, or brand names anywhere (uniforms, vans, tool brands, packaging). No AI tells (mangled hands, warped pipes/threads).
- Consistent color world across the whole set: cool steel/copper/water tones + warm wood/skin/brass accents. Sites are tinted navy/brass by default — images should sit well under a subtle dark overlay.
